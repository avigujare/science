How does producing Wind Energy impact the enviroment?

Producing wind energy is mostly good for the environment because it’s clean and doesn’t cause pollution like burning coal or oil does. It also helps slow down climate change and keeps the air fresh because it doesn’t release harmful gases. Plus, wind is renewable, which means we’ll never run out of it. But there are some downsides too. Building wind turbines uses a lot of materials like metal and concrete, which can cause pollution when they’re made. Wind farms can also harm animals like birds and bats that might fly into the blades by accident. Some people don’t like how wind turbines look because they think they ruin the view, and they can be a bit noisy too. Even with these problems, wind energy is still way better for the planet than using fossil fuels, and scientists are finding ways to make it even safer and quieter!

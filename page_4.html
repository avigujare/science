How does using Wind energy impact the enviroment?

Using wind energy is mostly good for the environment because it’s clean and doesn’t pollute the air. Unlike burning coal or oil, wind energy doesn’t release harmful gases that cause climate change. It also uses wind, which is renewable and will never run out. But there are some downsides too. Wind turbines can hurt birds and bats if they fly into the blades. Building the turbines also uses a lot of materials, which can cause pollution when they’re made. Some people think wind farms look ugly and don’t like the noise the turbines make. Still, wind energy is way better for the environment than fossil fuels, and people are working on making it even safer for nature!

How can we reduce its impact on the inviroment?

We can reduce the impact of wind energy on the environment by making a few changes. One way is by protecting wildlife—for example, placing wind turbines in areas where birds and bats don’t fly, so they don’t get hurt by the blades. We can also use better materials to build the turbines, ones that don’t cause as much pollution when they’re made. Another idea is to make the turbines quieter so they don’t bother people or animals nearby. Plus, we can recycle the parts of old turbines instead of throwing them away, so they don’t just end up in a landfill. If we do these things, we can make wind energy even better for the environment!
